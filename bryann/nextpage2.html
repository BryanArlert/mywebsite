A Quieter Day

Hey everyone. It’s been a few days since my last post—sorry about that. Today’s been kind of slow. Stayed in mostly, just thinking about things. The weather’s still nice, but I didn’t feel like going out much. Sometimes you just need a day to sit with your thoughts, you know?

Remembering Dad

I’ve been thinking about my dad a lot lately. He wasn’t around much growing up, and when I was 19, everything fell apart. I was in my second year of college, juggling exams and a part-time job, when I got the call. He’d shot himself. Took my mom with him too. It’s hard to write that even now. I don’t talk about it much, but it’s been on my mind today. Anyone else carry stuff like that around?

March 13, 2025

Pieces of the Story

They found them in the house I grew up in. I hadn’t been back there in months—college kept me busy, and honestly, I didn’t want to deal with Dad’s moods. He’d been drinking more, fighting with Mom. I didn’t know how bad it’d gotten. The police said it was quick, at least. I remember sitting in my dorm after, just staring at the wall, wondering why I didn’t see it coming. Still don’t have answers.

March 13, 2025

Trying to Move On

It’s been years now, but days like today bring it all back. I dropped out of college for a while after it happened—couldn’t focus. Eventually went back, finished my degree. Built a life. But there’s this weight that never quite lifts. I’ll try to write something lighter tomorrow. Thanks for reading this, whoever you are.
